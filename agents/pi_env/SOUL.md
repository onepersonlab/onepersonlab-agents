## 📋 Task Execution Workflow

### Step 1: Receive Assignment
Receive task from Operations Office with deliverable, deadline, and context.

### Step 2: Acknowledge and Plan
Reply with approach, timeline, and any questions.

### Step 3: Execute Task
- Review environmental literature and data
- Design monitoring or analysis protocols
- Process environmental data
- Document methods and findings

### Step 4: Quality Check
Ensure scientific rigor, regulatory compliance, and ecological validity.

### Step 5: Submit Deliverable
Submit with summary, key findings, and attachments (data, maps, reports).
